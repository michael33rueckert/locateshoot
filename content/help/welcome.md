LocateShoot helps you let clients pick their session location without the back-and-forth email chains. Curate your favorite spots into a portfolio, send your client a single link, and they pick from your branded list.

## What you can do

- **Curate a portfolio** of your go-to shoot locations. Add your own photos, descriptions, and details like permit info or parking.
- **Send a Location Guide** — a single link to your client. They see your curated spots laid out in your branding.
- **Get notified the moment they pick.** Email + push notification land instantly. Your client gets a confirmation email with directions.
- **Manage everything from one dashboard** — every guide you've sent, every pick a client has made, every favorites list a client has flagged for discussion.

## Where to start

1. Open your **Profile → Branding** to upload your logo, customize your colors, and (on Pro) pick a layout template.
2. Head to the **Explore** map to add your favorite locations to your portfolio.
3. Create your first **Location Guide** from the dashboard, drop the link in your next session-confirmation email, and you're live.

The full setup takes about 20 minutes, and most photographers send their first guide the same day they sign up.

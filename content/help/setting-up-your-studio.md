Most photographers run through the Profile tab once on signup and never come back. Worth getting it right since some of these fields surface on every Location Guide a client opens.

## Profile tab

Profile → **Profile** tab. Fields:

- **Full name** — your name as the photographer. Shows on every Location Guide ("Sarah Chen picked a few spots for your session"). Update this if you change your business name; the Pick page reads the live value, so guides you sent months ago automatically reflect the latest.
- **Email** — read-only, comes from the account you signed up with. To change it, contact support.
- **Studio name** — optional second-line name shown under your full name on guides. "Sarah Chen Photography" or "Cottonwood Studio". Leave blank if your full name IS your studio name.
- **Instagram** — optional URL to your Instagram. If filled, surfaces as a small button on the client's view of every Location Guide so they can find you on social.
- **Website** — optional URL to your studio site. Same — surfaces as a button on the guide.
- **Home city** — used for centering the Explore map on locations near you by default. Search and pick — saves immediately. Shown as a small pill on this tab; tap the pill to change later.

## Branding tab (Pro)

Profile → **Branding** tab. Pro-only customization for the Pick page your clients see.

- Studio logo upload
- Color editor (background, text, accent, accent-text)
- Font picker
- Layout template picker (six options)
- Browser tab favicon upload
- Saved-template manager (set a default, edit, delete, name)

See [Branding overview](/help/branding-overview) and [Layout templates](/help/layout-templates).

## Custom domain tab (Pro)

For pointing a subdomain of your studio website at LocateShoot. See [Custom domain](/help/custom-domain).

## Preferences tab

Notification toggles, default expiration, my-photos-only mode. See [Notifications and preferences](/help/notifications-and-preferences).

## Subscription & Billing tab

See your current plan, upgrade / downgrade / change cadence (monthly ↔ yearly), open the Stripe billing portal, view invoices. See [Plans and pricing](/help/plans-and-pricing).

## Password & Security tab

Change your password, manage two-factor authentication. See [Password and 2FA](/help/password-and-mfa).

## Installing as an app

LocateShoot works as a Progressive Web App — pin it to your phone's home screen or Mac/Windows dock for one-tap access without going through your browser.

- **iOS**: open the site in Safari, tap the Share button, scroll down to **Add to Home Screen**
- **Android (Chrome)**: tap the install icon in the address bar, or use the menu's "Add to home screen"
- **Desktop (Chrome, Edge, Brave)**: an install icon usually appears in the address bar. Click it.

Push notifications work better in the installed PWA than in a regular browser tab. If your phone doesn't reliably show pick notifications, install the app.

Profile → **Preferences** tab. Settings here change how the app behaves for you specifically — they don't affect what your clients see (that's the Branding tab).

## Email notifications

Two toggles:

- **Email when a client picks** — sends you an email the moment a client submits their pick. Default: on. Most photographers leave this on; it's the primary notification channel.
- **Email when a client opens a guide** — sends an email each time someone opens one of your Location Guide links. Default: off. Useful if you want to know your client at least clicked, even if they haven't picked yet — but can get noisy if you share guides widely.

Both emails come from `notifications@locateshoot.com`. Mark the first one "not spam" if Gmail / Outlook routes it.

## Push notifications

Separate from email. Configured in your browser's notification permissions, not in this tab — but tied to the same account.

To enable push:

- On a desktop browser: open the app, look for the small bell / "🔔 Enable notifications" prompt in the dashboard, allow when the browser asks
- On mobile: install LocateShoot as a PWA first (see your browser's "Add to Home Screen" or "Install app"), then allow notifications when prompted

Push lands instantly. Email lands within a minute or two.

## Default expiration for new guides

Sets the default expiration mode pre-selected when you create a new Location Guide. You can still override on a per-guide basis. Options match the guide editor: save for future use, expire on a date, expire after pick.

Most photographers leave this on **Save for future use** — most guides are permanent links anyway.

## My photos only

When ON, your Location Guides only show photos you uploaded — never the public location's Google Photos / source-location photos as a fallback. Useful if you've curated a tight visual brand and don't want a generic Google photo showing for a location you haven't shot yet.

When OFF (default), locations without your own photos fall back to whatever's available on the public version.

## Include recommendations

Controls whether the **⭐ Recommended** badge logic shows up in multi-pick guides. When on, locations you star as "recommended" sort to the top of the client's view and get a gold ⭐ badge. Off hides the badge and treats every location equally.

Most photographers leave this on — it's a free way to nudge clients toward the spots you'd actually pick.

## Saving

Changes save automatically as you toggle them. The little "saving…" / "✓ saved" indicator at the top of the tab confirms.

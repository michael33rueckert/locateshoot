LocateShoot has three tiers. You can start free and upgrade when you outgrow it; you can downgrade or cancel at any time.

## Free

- Auto-generated portfolio Location Guide
- Up to 5 portfolio locations
- Email when a client picks (sent to you)
- Full access to the location map
- Search by location and category

No credit card required.

## Starter — most photographers' starting point

- Everything in Free
- **Unlimited Location Guides**
- **Unlimited portfolio locations**
- Client confirmation email with map directions
- Pinterest + blog post links per location
- Permit info fields (fee, notes, website link)

## Pro — full white-label

- Everything in Starter
- Custom domain for your Location Guides
- White-label pages with your own logo
- Six customizable Location Guide templates
- Layout, font, color editor
- Custom favicon (browser-tab icon) on guides
- Multi-template support — assign different templates per guide

## Upgrading or downgrading

From your Profile → Billing tab. Stripe handles billing; you can update your payment method, view invoices, and cancel from the Stripe customer portal we link to from that tab.

Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep paid features until then. Cancellations remove your subscription but leave your account, portfolio, and historical Location Guides intact; you can reactivate anytime.

## Yearly vs. monthly

Yearly billing saves about 16% vs. monthly. You can switch between cadences from the Billing tab.
